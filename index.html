Yogi!

Hi! My name is Yogi. I’m a black German shepherd mix. I was born in Alabama and was adopted from the Atlanta humane society when I was 8 weeks old. I am two years old and always in high spirits. I am friendly to new people and dogs but will always be loyal to my pack.

Chance!

Hello! My name is Chance. I’m a Terrier mix. I was found tied to a propane tank. I’m around two years old and I love people. I am friendly, loyal, and show my gratitude when I get the chance. I am quiet but very intelligent and follow all commands.

Happiness!

We like to run in the backyard and wrestle. We think our life would be bland without each other as brothers. We do everything together including eating, playing, and tearing through shoes. We’re just the best!
